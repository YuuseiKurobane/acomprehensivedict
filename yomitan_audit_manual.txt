  many senses, homograph grouping, inline subentries, derived/related forms,
  labels, template expansion, and cross-reference navigation.

    related-form panels are visually separated and ordered correctly.
  - Click a representative Kata Dasar, Kata Turunan/Kata Terkait, and internal
    cross-reference link when present; confirm that the intended result opens.
  - Check that template dashes expand to the headword without unwanted spaces
    or swallowed hyphens.
  - Try the listed spelling exactly. For entries with stated aliases, also try
    those aliases and confirm that the visible definition still prints them.
